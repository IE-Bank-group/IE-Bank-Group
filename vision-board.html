Product Vision Board

Target Audience

  • Primary Users: Tech-savvy individuals, young professionals, and small business owners who value digital solutions for managing finances.
  • Secondary Users: General banking customers interested in accessing essential financial services online.

User Needs

  • Convenient Account Management: Users need easy access to view, manage, and transfer funds between accounts.
  • Secure Access: Users expect their data and transactions to be protected by robust security measures, including secure password storage and access control.
  • Simplicity and Efficiency: A straightforward, user-friendly interface that allows quick and seamless banking operations.

Product

  • Unique Value: IE Bank’s digital banking platform provides essential services with a focus on security and simplicity, enabling users to handle all basic financial transactions without needing to visit a physical branch.
  • Key Features: Includes an admin portal for bank user management, a user portal for account management, and secure transaction capabilities.

Business Goals

  • User Growth: Achieve a 30% increase in registered users within the first year post-launch.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Maintain a customer satisfaction rate of at least 85% through user-friendly design and consistent support.
  • Retention Rate: Reach a 75% active user retention rate within the first six months.
  • Operational Efficiency: Complete MVP testing and deployment with at least 90% of initial features functioning optimally.
  • Feedback Loop: Implement a structured feedback mechanism, achieving a 60% feedback response rate to continuously improve user experience.
