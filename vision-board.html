Product Vision Board

Target Audience

  • Primary Users: Digital individuals, young students, and small business owners who value digital banking solutions to managing their finances.
  • Secondary Users: Banking customers interested in accessing financial services online.

User Needs

  • Convenient Account Management: Users need easy access to view, manage, and transfer funds between accounts.
  • Secure Access: Users expect their data and transactions to be protected by critical security measures, including secure password storage and access control.
  • Simplicity and Efficiency: A user-friendly interface that allows quick banking operations.

Product

  • Unique Value: IE Bank’s digital banking platform provides financial services while focusing on security and simplicity, enabling users to handle all basic financial transactions easily.
  • Key Features: Includes an admin portal for bank user management, a user portal for account management, and secure transaction abilities.

Business Goals

  • User Growth: Achieve about a 30% increase in registered users within the first year after launch.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Maintain customer satisfaction rate of at least 80% through maintaing a user-friendly design and providing consistent support.
  • Retention Rate: Reach a 75% active user retention rate within the first six-eight months.
  • Operational Efficiency: Complete MVP testing and deployment with at least 90% of initial features functioning.
  • Feedback Loop: Implement a well-structured feedback mechanism.
